Register a death

There are 2 steps to registering a death:

  1. Give information about the person and yourself – you can do that online using the link and reference number below.
  2. Make an appointment with our registrars to sign paperwork and order death certificates.

You can check and change your answers at the interview. If you prefer, you can answer the questions at the interview.

What you'll need

You cannot save and return to your answers, so it’s a good idea to have all the information before you start.

You’ll need their:

  • full name, and any previous names
  • date and place of birth
  • place of death
  • last address
  • occupation, or last occupation if they were retired
  • spouse or civil partner's full name, date of birth and occupation
