There are 2 steps to registering a death:

  1. Give information about the person and yourself.
  2. Make an appointment with a registrar to sign paperwork and order death certificates.

You can give some or all of the registration information now and sign the register in person. Or you can answer all the questions during the registration appointment.

What you'll need

You'll need information about their:

  • full name, and any previous names
  • usual address
  • occupation, or last occupation if they were retired
  • date and place of birth
  • date and place of death
  • spouse or civil partner's full name, date of birth and occupation, if relevant

You cannot save and return to your answers in this form, but you'll be able to amend information in the registration appointment, if you need to.
